Traditional Tuna Onigiri

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Rice, Japanese Short Grain
  • Salt
  • Rice paper or seaweed wrap

Directions

  1. Prepare rice per instructions on container
  2. Season, bake, and shred tuna...or by prepackaged
  3. While rice is still hot fold tuna into central pocket and mold into desired shape
  4. Wrap rice with ricepaper or seaweed as desired
